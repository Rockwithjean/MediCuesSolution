About Us

Medi Cues Medical Solutions

Medi Cues Solutions Limited is a trusted supplier of medical equipment, medical accessories, medical consumables, medical consultancy, equipment clinical application training, and medical equipment service. We work with the best global brands to deliver high quality equipment, accessories, spare parts, consumables and service to our customers since our inception in 2024.

We are located at Kilimanjaro City Arcade, Garden Estate, Nairobi, Kenya. We offer a comprehensive range of medical equipment, accessories, spare parts, consumables and service that cater to all your hospital needs, ensuring that your facility runs smoothly.

Hundreds of Satisfied Customers

We take pride in having served hundreds of satisfied customers with high-quality medical equipment, accessories, hospital consumables, clinical training and equipment service.

Highly Skilled Specialists
